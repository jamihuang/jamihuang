About Me

Welcome to my website!

This is the personal website of Jami, an eighth grade girl going to school at Rumsey Hall School, which is an American junior boarding school. Learn more about me scrolling down.

I am an extrovert girl who loves all kind of sports, photography, and is passionate for the things that I do. I have went to international schools since kindergarten, and had experiences with sports at a very young age. I also have a younger brother, and a cat named Leo. Here, my sports experiences, photography work, writing pieces, and some daily life at school are recorded.

Sports

Here are some of the sports I do

Tennis

Tennis is definitely my favorite sport and one that I that I am pretty good at. The first time I played tennis was during summer vacation of my fifth grade year, but I have been through a lot of ups and downs with the sport. The time I really started practicing with all my efforts and time was April 2020. I spent three hours a day, sometimes even more, on practicing tennis during this summer. I love it very much and am excited to see where I can get with it.

Tennis Photos and Videos

Crew

Crew is another sport that I love a lot. Although I did not spend as much time as tennis on crew, I would say that I enjoyed it a lot during the short time I practiced it. I was introduced to crew at the beginning of summer this year, and practice for two hours every Friday and Saturday of the week. It is very fun rowing in the lake with teammates and is a great way of exercising.

Crew Photos and Videos

Figure Skating

My feelings about figure skating would always be very complicated and mixed. I am confident in saying that I was better at figure skating then than I am at tennis and crew right now combined. I trained for skating a lot in the years I practiced it, and got really good at it. I hated it though, because it was always so tiring and inhumane. I received a lot of awards on figure skating, including being ranked 13th in the national championships and passing the 8th level out of 10 levels. I had to give up skating in May 2018 because of a knee injury.

Figure Skating Photos and Videos

Field Hockey and Ice Hockey

Field hockey and ice hockey are sports I started practicing last year at school in the United States. Because of my great basis on ice skating, I trusted myself with adjusting easily to ice hockey. I was able to practice ice hockey, and joined the varsity girls hockey team at school. Field hockey season in the Fall was definitely my favorite season. I had never even heard of field hockey before when I was in China, but decided to try this new sport at Rumsey. My teachers and teammates were very supportive, and although I wasn't able to make it into the varisty team, I was chosen as captain in the varisty B girls field hockey team.
