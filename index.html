About Me

Welcome to my website!

This is the personal website of Jami, an eighth grade girl going to school at Rumsey Hall School, which is an American junior boarding school. Learn more about me scrolling down.

I am an extravert girl who loves all kind of sports, photography, and is passionate for the things that I do. I have went to international schools since kindergarten, and had experiences with sports at a very young age. I also have a younger brother, and a cat named Leo. Here, my sports experiences, photography work, writing pieces, and some daily life at school are recorded.
